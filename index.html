About Cordillera

Cordillera Heritage & Eco-Tourism

The Cordillera is a mountain region in Northern Luzon known for its rice terraces, rich culture, and breathtaking landscapes. Local communities have lived in harmony with nature for centuries, showing how tradition and sustainability go hand in hand.

Eco-tourism here means more than sightseeing—it's about trekking the peaks, learning from indigenous heritage, and supporting locals while helping protect the environment.

Visiting the Cordillera lets you enjoy its beauty while being part of preserving it for future generations.
